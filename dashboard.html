Anatomy-aside, the best bicep workouts and exercises allow you to increase your muscle mass, strength, stability and aesthetic appeal.

Chest

The chest muscles are responsible for moving the arms across the body and up and down, as well as other movements like flexion, adduction, and rotation.

Back

Back muscles are the main structural support for your trunk (torso). These muscles help you move your body, including your head, neck, shoulders, arms and legs.

Shoulder

Shoulder muscles stabilize your shoulder joint and help you move your arm in many directions.

Tricep

Triceps muscle acts to hold the head of the humerus in the glenoid cavity. This action helps prevent any displacement of the humerus.

legs

Strong leg muscles keep your body balanced, which isn't possible if you focus solely on your upper body.
